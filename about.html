Our story

It was in 2013 that a spark of inspiration set the ball rolling for the establishment of CGB Solutions. The next year was spent in research, networking, and strategizing, laying the foundation for what was to follow. By 2015, small-scale IT solutions started taking concrete shape, marking the beginning of the company's journey towards existence. With credibility increasing among official clients in 2016, expertise in enterprise IT and automation increased in 2017. Momentum grew consistently, with more robust partnerships and streamlined solutions in 2018, opening the door for a robust portfolio and global aspirations by 2019.

CGB Solutions was officially launched in 2020, revolutionizing industries with innovation. Key collaborations and large-scale projects drove expansion in 2021, which in turn created industry-wide growth in 2022. By 2023, the business had reached new heights, with operations in the USA and Dubai. Now, in 2024, CGB Solutions is on an unrelenting rise, with 1,400+ consultants powering revolutionary projects globally.
