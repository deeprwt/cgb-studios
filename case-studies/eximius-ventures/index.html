CAMPAIGN OVERVIEW

Boost In Traffic and Search Visibility

Throughout the campaign, Eximius Venture experienced remarkable improvements in its organic presence and website performance. By addressing technical issues, creating high-quality content, and optimizing the site for relevant keywords, we achieved substantial growth in traffic, engagement, and search visibility.

Organic Traffic Surge

The number of monthly organic visitors increased from 0 in March 2024 to 7359 by September 2024. This represents an astounding 73,000% increase in organic traffic, demonstrating the impact of a targeted SEO strategy. The steady traffic growth not only brought more visibility but also attracted more qualified leads to the site.

Improved Backlink Profile

Starting with only 358 backlinks, we saw a gradual increase to 485 backlinks by September 2024, a 35% increase. This increase in backlinks came from targeted outreach to industry blogs and publications, bolstering the site’s credibility and authority in the venture capital space. These links were sourced from reputable, high-authority domains, which contributed to higher search rankings for critical keywords.

On-Page SEO Score Enhancement

The on-page SEO score improved from 71 in March 2024 to 96 by September 2024, a 35% increase. This improvement was driven by thorough optimization of meta tags, headings, internal linking, and content updates. Fixing technical SEO issues also played a vital role in achieving a higher score, which in turn positively affected search engine rankings.

Keyword Growth

By focusing on strategic keyword targeting in the venture capital sector, we saw a substantial increase in the number of indexed organic keywords. The blog library and content calendar development ensured that we targeted a wider array of relevant search queries, helping drive both short-tail and long-tail keyword rankings.

Increased Impressions and Clicks

The overall impressions and clicks saw a significant rise as the content became more visible in SERPs (Search Engine Results Pages). The cumulative SEO efforts resulted in higher click-through rates (CTR) for targeted pages, increasing both site engagement and lead generation opportunities.
